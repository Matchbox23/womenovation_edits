OUR STORY

it all started when we noticed that...

75% of Egyptian women eligible to work are

NOT actively participating in the economy

Compared to men doing the same job,

women earn 34% LESS

For thousands of years, Egyptian women have been writing history and until today, they still inspire the world. We believe in the power of these women's talent to elevate our society culturally and economically.

Building on our history, we selected a mandate that...

CREATING MORE SPACE FOR WOMEN TO...

LEARN FLOURISH & INNOVATE

The power of art and design is evident in all disciplines of life. Art and design are able to visualize, fictionalize, express feelings, move emotions and represent identities, which gives them an exceptional power to reach and communicate. Art narrates stories, documents history, commemorates legends to construct timeless heritage generations that follow. At Womenovation, our artistic focus, appreciation of design and concern about heritage is a reflection of our future-oriented vision and a strategic approach prioritizing the quality of life of upcoming generations.

OUR FOCUS ON EMPOWERING WOMEN, CONCERN ABOUT OUR HERITAGE & INTENTION TO DEVELOP GREENER INNOVATIVE INDUSTRIES
