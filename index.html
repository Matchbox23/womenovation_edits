ABOUT US

The Womenovation Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to accelerate sustainable development through a specificfocus on gender. Our strategy utilizes creative industries indeveloping economic opprtunities for women through creating entrepreneurship and decent work opportunities. We also collaborate with creative artists to generate content and curte events that advocate for empowering women and strengthen thevalue of gender equality in the society.
