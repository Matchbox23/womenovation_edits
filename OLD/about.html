About us

The Womenovation Foundation was launched in November 2022 during COP27’s Gender Day in Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt. The Foundation’s concept emerged with an aim to accelerate sustainable development through growing the economy by providing more economic opportunities for women and effectively promoting the innovative outputs created. Our strategy believes in the power of advocacy and effective utilization of media, culture, arts, and innovation to promote an innovative culture believing in gender equality.

Our targets are coherent to the United Nations’ global Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). However, due to the broad nature of SDGs, we rely on evidence-based research that identify community challenges and an in-depth overview of local needs. Research results assist us in setting priorities and delivering projects with stronger impact. We are currently active through our projects are based in Egypt as an initial steppingstone towards future expansion in Africa and the Middle East.

What we do

Sustainable development without effective advocacy and awareness is inevitable. Most Development cooperation projects constantly lose opportunities of impact optimization due to their limited focus on citizen engagement, advocacy, and communication. Our existence is driven by an aim to fill this gap through integrating fine and applied arts in sustainable development. We design programs based on bottom-up and top-down approaches depending on the development objectives. The Foundation’s approach is based on partnerships and community collaborations motivated to utilize artistic tools to positively engage communities in sustainable development.

How we do it

Connect

We connect and partner with institutions and youth networks to identify talented and innovative mindsets to be hired as interns. Meanwhile, we partner with startups and entrepreneurs with high potential to grow, but incapable to implement or design efficient plans to grow. We then match talented individuals from our youth networks with entrepreneurs to develop integrated working teams under our supervision.

Inspire

We identify challenges derived from these working groups and provide them with relevant expertise needed to overcome these challenges. Experts have a role to inspire working groups and empower them in overcoming their challenges. Inspiration is delivered through sharing success stories, trainings, workshops, and generating ideas through creative thinking.

Innovate

Entrepreneurs and interns are on the right track and able to perform more efficiently. They have space for creativity, they developed new skills and ready to elevate their work to a mature stage that provides customers with a higher quality, they can widen their clients’ base and increase their revenue. They can innovate.

Advocate

We utilize our governmental, private sector and civil society partnerships in promoting innovation aiming to strengthen our national industrial capacities and maximizing opportunities to export and gain higher foreign investment.

Philosophy

Combining “women” and “innovation” is a statement expressing our belief in the role gender equality and innovation play in enriching local culture, fostering economic growth, and promoting sustainable development.

Are men excluded?

NO!, The word “woman” includes ‘man’, the word “women” includes ‘men’ and “female” includes ‘male’. Using “women” in our branding is a continuous reminder of the crucial need to empower women in societies aiming for comprehensive gender equality. We tend to support more women as they do get less chances on the ground.

What Innovation?

We discover talents and guide our upcoming stars to optimize their talent, we enrich their knowledge these talents to develop innovative solutions that serve the sustainable development agenda. Their work is evaluated by us and policy makers and integrate their innovation in the national development process.

Added value

Young talents gain a wealthy experience and broaden their horizon through exposure to sustainable development and the dynamic interactive business environment at emerging companies. Their development or enhancement of soft skills and gaining new information is our share of investing in the future. Integrating art in our work enhances their ability to understand and visualize existing or potential challenges is our method to encourage out-of-the-box ideas and multi-dimensional thinking.
Our approach is an opportunity to obtain more inclusive and representative insights that later formulate a more defined image of their perception. Collecting insights and identifying misconceptions evolve to understand public opinion leading to more efficient decision making. Moreover, We value Ideas generated by young innovators as they tend to have a more futuristic character.. Existence of contributions with high potential could expand into bigger projects with possibilities of macro-level benefits and returns.
