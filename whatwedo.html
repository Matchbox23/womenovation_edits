CIIA MODEL FOR DESIGN ENTREPRENEURS

CIIA is a business development learning model developed by Womenovation aiming to support entrepreneurs in design-sensitive industries. The model is constructed in a quadrilateral structure representing four phases. Each phase is designed to fulfill certain objectives based on teamwork and collaborations. Through the four stages, entrepreneurs are able to identify their strengths and challenges and are expected develop a “one industry” attitude driven by realizing the economic benefits of industrial collaboration.The model is explained as follows.

CONNECT

We connect talented designers to industry professionals, workshops, creative talents in supporting fields (e.g. photographers & art directors), investors, strategic events, donors, Through these networks, we organize workshops, work with talented students to brainstorm and generate ideas based on win-win scenarios.

INSPIRE

When connections and collaborations are formed, we aim for interactive workshops, creative thinking sessions that aim for “iinspiration” as an outcome. We believe that creative talents from diverse fields, generations, education, and career backgrounds are able to generate more efficient planning and guidance for business development.

INNOVATE

An enriched inspirational experience based on creative thinking allows for talented designers and design entrepreneurs to clearly identify their artistic direction, strategy, short term objectives and overall vision for the future. Before launching newly innovated products, we test the products through a sample of our target audiences to ensure our planning was accurate and minimize any risk of failure.

ADVOCATE

The successful launch of an innovative product range should derive positive results in terms of revenue and feedbasck. We therefore expect yielding positive results at this phase. As unexpected obstacles and results are always a possibility, the phase transforms to “evaluate” , a phase revising the overall process to identify the root cause planned expectations.

THE EGYPTIAN WOMEN’S MUSEUM

During its launch event, Womenovation presented the concept of establishing the first Egyptian Women’s Museum aiming to:

  • 1- Promote gender equality through a storytelling approach focusing onsuccess stories of women in Egypt’s contemporary history.
  • 2- Add a state-of-the-art cultural space as a valuable contribution to the localcontemporary art scene.
  • 3- Provide capacity-building services for women focusing on entrepreneurshipand business development.
  • 4- Provide talented women with an opportunity to display their talent throughthe museum shop, museum restaurant, art exhibitions, bazaars, etc.
